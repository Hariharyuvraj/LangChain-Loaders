Cricket is one of the most popular sports in the world.

It is played between two teams of eleven players each.

The game is played with a bat, a ball, and wickets.

One team bats while the other team bowls and fields.

The batting team scores runs by hitting the ball and running between wickets.

The bowling team tries to get the batsmen out.

A match is usually played in formats like Test, One-Day, or T20.

Cricket needs teamwork, skill, and strategy.

In India, cricket is often called a religion because of its huge fan base.

Great players like Sachin Tendulkar and Virat Kohli have made the game more famous.
